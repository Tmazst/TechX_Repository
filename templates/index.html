Appenda


Appenda is a platform where users (EmaSwati) can upload images of any kind that showcase the beauty of Eswatini. These images highlight the scenic wonders of our country, including its mountains, unique vegetation, rivers, roads, aerial views, schools, natural springs, cultural attire, custom-made designs, and locally created paintings. The primary goal of this platform is to display and celebrate the beauty of Eswatini, serving as an image resource center for various projects. A key objective for the near future is to create a source of passive income for users who upload their images, which can be available for free or with an option to contribute to owner of the image.

TechConnect+


TechConnect Plus is a platform that serves as a repository where users (EmaSwati) can easily discover and explore local mobile applications that cater to their service requirements. The web app also aims to provide basic quality assurance to uphold the integrity of the mobile application ecosystem by offering developers technical assistance to address bugs, security vulnerabilities, and other potential flaws. This initiative will help ensure that applications meet high-quality standards and that first-time users do not encounter these technical issues.
